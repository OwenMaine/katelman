Fabrication Protocols

A detailed schematic of our core system capabilities.

01 / Structural Steel

We are experts in the fabrication of heavy structural steel for commercial, industrial, and residential projects. Our facility is equipped to handle complex frameworks that form the backbone of modern architecture.

  • Beams, Columns, and Trusses
  • Mezzanines and Equipment Platforms
  • Custom Support Structures

02 / Stairs & Rails

We combine safety, functionality, and aesthetics to create custom stairs, handrails, and guardrails. From intricate ornamental designs to heavy-duty industrial access, we fabricate solutions that meet all codes and client specifications.

  • Monumental and Egress Stairs
  • ADA-Compliant Handrails
  • Decorative and Industrial Guardrails

03 / Reinforcing

Strength starts from within. We supply and fabricate steel reinforcing bars (rebar) that provide the essential tensile strength for concrete structures, ensuring the integrity and longevity of foundations, walls, and slabs.

  • Rebar Cutting and Bending
  • Pre-Assembled Cages
  • Stock Length Supply
