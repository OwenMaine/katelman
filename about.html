Our History

1998 - Foundation

Katelman Steel was founded with a mission to bring superior structural fabrication to the Midwest, starting with a small workshop and a dedicated team.

2005 - Major Expansion

We expanded our facility, incorporating advanced machinery and doubling our capacity to take on larger and more complex projects across Iowa.

Today - Industry Leader

As a recognized leader in steel fabrication, we continue to innovate, providing structural steel, stairs, and rails built on a legacy of strength and precision.
